FAQ

What are Bible Bricks?

Bible Bricks is a custom LEGO-compatible set designed to help people engage with Biblical stories in a hands-on, visual way. These sets are crafted to inspire deeper understanding and create memorable experiences during family time, youth group meetings, homeschool lessons, Bible study groups, and more.

Are the bricks compatible with LEGO?

Yes! Our sets are made with high-quality, LEGO-compatible bricks, so you can easily integrate them with your existing LEGO collection. This ensures a lower cost while maintaining durability and a satisfying building experience.

What is included in each set?

Each set includes all the bricks you need to build a Biblical scene, including minifigures, detailed instructions, and decal stickers. The instructions also include relevant Bible verses at various stages of the building process to help you learn the symbolic significance of the parts you're constructing.

How can I use Bible Bricks?

Bible Bricks is versatile and can be used in many ways:

  • Family Recreation: Build together and spark meaningful conversations about faith.
  • Youth Group Programs: Create an interactive and memorable biblical learning experience.
  • Homeschool Curriculum: Hands-on history and spiritual education.
  • Bible Study Groups: Use as a visual aid for deeper theological discussions.
  • Vacation Bible School: Engage kids in an unforgettable learning tool.
  • Missionary Training: Understand biblical symbolism through building.
  • Personal Devotional Study: Take a tactile approach to exploring scripture.

What age group is Bible Bricks suitable for?

Bible Bricks sets are designed for a wide range of ages. Younger builders can enjoy the hands-on activity, while older participants can engage in the deeper lessons tied to the biblical stories.
